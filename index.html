About Me

I’m Petter Køltzow, a service design student at Kristiania University College. I combine creative thinking with human-centered design principles to craft solutions that don’t just meet today’s needs, but anticipate tomorrow’s challenges. Design should be functional, beautiful, and impactful, and I strive to achieve this with every project.

Solving Wicked Problems

One of my greatest strengths within the service design profession is my ability and commitment to solving wicked problems

Thinking Outside The Box

For the coming generations to have a future, change must occur either by design or disaster. I vote for doing it through design and i therefore have a passion for thinking new and outside the box.

Always Challenging Myself

To think new, one must dare to challenge the current standard of what is possible and what is not possible. That's why I love to explore the unexplored, and take every challenge I get head on.
