Scenario Development

This project takes the reader through a process of developing scenarios for service design. The journey will explore key steps and decisions made throughout the project.

Solving a problem on campus Fjerdingen

Discover

The project began with initial research and understanding the problem space. This phase helped frame the objectives clearly and laid the groundwork for further development.

We conducted stakeholder interviews to gather insights, and these findings became crucial in shaping the future scenarios for the service.

After compiling all insights, we proceeded to brainstorm and map out potential directions for the service based on real user needs and feedback.

Define

With initial ideas in place, we ran several co-creation workshops with key stakeholders to refine and validate our assumptions.

During these workshops, we explored various future scenarios, considering multiple perspectives from users and service providers alike.

Develop

Following the workshops, we analyzed the data and feedback, using it to refine the most promising service scenarios.

This led to the development of a set of potential future scenarios, each grounded in the real needs and desires of users.

Deliver

We concluded the process by selecting the most viable scenario, one that aligned with both user expectations and business goals.
